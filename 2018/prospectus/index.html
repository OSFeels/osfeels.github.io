Why Sponsor OS&F?

Open Source & Feelings (OS&F) is a conference dedicated to authentically designing and reshaping a supportive, inclusive, open source culture—to uncover and implement design patterns for a vibrant, skilled, and inclusive community.

In 2018, we are producing the third iteration of Open Source & Feelings, and we invite you to be a part of this amazing community event. Independent, community-focused events like OS&F cannot happen without the supporters like you.

Your sponsorship makes possible our most lofty goals for 2018, including doubling compensation for our presenters and building an attendee grant program to assist potential attendees with all manner of costs, including tickets, travel, childcare, and more.

Measure the impact

By sponsoring Open Source & Feelings, your organization makes a positive, lasting impression with hundreds of Open Source community members. All of whom have a keen interest in making the tech sector a more inclusive and empathetic community!
